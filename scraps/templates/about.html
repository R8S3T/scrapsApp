About @Scraps

@Scraps is a platform designed for users to trade, exchange, or give away items they no longer need. Our goal is to help people find new homes for their unused or unwanted items while fostering a community of sharing and environmental responsibility.

Features

  • Easy registration and login process
  • Profile customization and management
  • Listing of items for trade or exchange
  • Interactive map for locating items and users nearby
  • Secure messaging system for communication between users

We hope you enjoy using @Scraps and encourage you to share it with your friends and family. Together, we can make a difference and contribute to a more sustainable future.
